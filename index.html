About Nine Heavens

Ascend through a living cultivation RPG.

Deep in the mortal realm, cultivators chase the same dream: ascend through the Nine Heavens and become something more than human.

Nine Heavens is a persistent, server-wide cultivation RPG inspired by Xianxia novels. Progress through cultivation stages from the lowly Mortal ranks through Mahayana, Immortal, and Sovereign, earning combat stats, unlocking flames, and breaking through realms at the risk of injury or demotion if heavenly tribulations crush your attempt.

Every breakthrough can raise your legend or send you back wounded.

Cultivate & Battle

Grow Qi, equip weapons, armor, and accessories with set bonuses and grade mastery, then challenge other players in PvP for glory and rewards.

Beasts & Pets

Hunt monsters, hatch and combine eggs, and raise a beast companion whose stats scale with your bond and its rarity.

Flames & Alchemy

Swallow flames to boost your cultivation, refine pills, and unlock rare crafting bonuses for the long climb upward.

Loot & Storage

Manage inventory and storage rings with real weight limits, open chests and mystery boxes, and route overflow loot when your ring runs full.

Economy & Auctions

Trade with spirit stones and martial wealth through a live escrow-backed auction house with automatic refunds and taxes on outbid offers.

Living World

Grind toward Sovereign, chase perfect quest rewards, and leave your mark inside a cultivation world that keeps moving with the server.

Realm Codex

Every stage on the path beyond mortality.

I

Mortal Body

1st - 9th Stage

The foundation of the cultivation journey. Cultivators refine physical blood, bone, and skin to prepare for drawing in active Qi.

Tribulation: None Success Rate: 100% Perks: Unlocks Questing & Daily Missions
II

Qi Gathering

1st - 9th Stage

Drawing spiritual energy from the environment into the dantian to form the first strands of active Qi.

Tribulation: None Success Rate: 90% Perks: Unlocks PvP Arena & Basic Gear
III

Foundation Establishment

Early, Middle, Late, Peak

Compressing Qi into a liquid spiritual sea, hardening meridians to anchor the cultivation path against deviance.

Tribulation: Qi Deviation Risk (10%) Success Rate: 75% Perks: Unlocks Alchemy, Herb Gathering & Pills
IV

Core Formation

Early, Middle, Late, Peak

Condensing the liquid spiritual sea into a solid Golden Core, achieving a major leap in longevity and combat strength.

Tribulation: Fire Tribulation Success Rate: 60% Perks: Unlocks Beast Companions & Hatching
V

Nascent Soul

Early, Middle, Late, Peak

Shattering the Golden Core to hatch a miniature spiritual infant, gaining telepathy, spiritual senses, and soul flight.

Tribulation: Five-Fold Lightning Tribulation Success Rate: 45% Perks: Unlocks Custom Flames & Spirit Refining
VI

Soul Transformation

Early Soul Transformation, Void Refinement, Spirit Severing, Peak Soul Transformation

Merging the physical body with the soul to achieve spiritual fusion and void refinement, transcending material constraints.

Tribulation: Heavenly Tribulation Success Rate: 30% Perks: Unlocks Gear Set Bonuses & Master Grades
VII

Mahayana

Body Mahayana, Soul Integration, Dao Integration, Peak Mahayana

Integrating the soul with the Dao itself, preparing to step beyond the boundary of mortality into divine realms.

Tribulation: 9-Fold Lightning Tribulation Success Rate: 20% Perks: Unlocks Escrow Auction & Rare Artifacts
VIII

Immortal Realm

True Immortal, Golden Immortal, Immortal King, Immortal Emperor

Transcending mortality entirely to gain eternal life, stepping onto the ladder of true deities and celestial kings.

Tribulation: Immortal Tribulation Success Rate: 10% Perks: Unlocks Sect Creation & Spiritual Veins
IX

Sovereign Realm

Celestial Sovereign, Primordial Sovereign, Ancestral Sovereign, Heaven Dao Sovereign, Unknown

Gaining absolute mastery over the laws of the universe, standing at the apex of the Nine Heavens as a primordial force.

Tribulation: Chaos Dao Tribulation Success Rate: 3% Perks: Unlocks Primordial Dominion & Laws
